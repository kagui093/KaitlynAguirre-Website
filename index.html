Political Science

My name is Kaitlyn Aguirre, and I am a second-year student at the University of California, Riverside. I am majoring in Political Science and will graduate in the Spring of 2025.

My interests and hobbies in school are the UC Riverside Mock Trial team and the Panhellenic Sorority Alpha Delta Pi. I currently hold leadership positions in both clubs. I serve as the President of the student-run Highlander Mock Trial Team where I coach fellow club members on all things law related such as how to write legal arguments, object, cross-examine a witness, and more. I serve as the Chapter Foundation Ambassador in my sorority, Alpha Delta Pi. As the chapter foundation ambassador, I act as a liaison between our school’s chapter and the headquarters of the sorority.

Outside school, I volunteer at the Norton Air Force Base Museum in San Bernardino, California. At the museum, I serve as a tour guide giving people tours of the museum and educating them on the history of the United States Air Force. Additionally, I educate visitors on the history of the Air Force’s presence in the city of San Bernardino.
